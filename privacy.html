Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Local Storage

Last Path stores your game progress, unlocked levels, settings, and high scores locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is used only to provide game functionality — it is not uploaded to our servers.

2. Internet Access

An internet connection may be required for displaying advertisements, loading rewarded ads, analytics reporting, and future online features. Most gameplay can be enjoyed without an active internet connection.

3. Advertisements

This game uses Google AdMob to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect and process information such as device identifiers, advertising IDs, IP address, approximate location, and other technical information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy to provide, personalize, and measure advertisements.

4. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Google Firebase Analytics and related Google services to collect anonymous or aggregated information about gameplay, feature usage, performance, crashes, and diagnostics. This information helps us improve game stability, fix bugs, and enhance the overall player experience.

5. Children's Privacy

If this game is made available to children, advertisements and data processing will follow the Google Play Families Policy and applicable laws. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information directly from children.

6. Third-Party Services

This game integrates third-party services including Google AdMob and Google Firebase. These services have their own privacy policies governing how they collect and process data, linked above.

7. Your Choices

You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads through your device's system settings at any time. Uninstalling the game removes all locally stored data from your device.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the game after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Significant changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our support page.
