About the game

One rule. A hundred ways to break it.

Last Path strips puzzle gaming down to a single, honest mechanic — and lets the boards do the rest of the talking.

One tap, one direction

Every arrow points somewhere. Tap it and it slides that way — simple to learn, but the order you choose is everything.

Boards with shape

Every puzzle is drawn into a real silhouette — letters, numbers, hourglasses, kites. Clearing the board reveals the shape.

Three lives, real stakes

Guess wrong and a blocked arrow costs a life. There's no timer pressure to panic over — just the board, and your read of it.
